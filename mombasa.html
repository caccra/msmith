Our Mombasa Office

M-Smith Advocates Mombasa serves clients in Kenya's Coast Region, Mombasa, Kilifi, Kwale, and surrounding counties. Our Mombasa team handles corporate transactions, property conveyancing, immigration compliance, and commercial disputes, with a particular understanding of the coastal land tenure landscape and the commercial dynamics of Kenya's main port city.

For clients operating across the East African corridor, connecting Uganda through Kenya to the Port of Mombasa, our Mombasa office provides the legal continuity that cross-border supply chains and logistics businesses require.

What We Handle

Legal Services Available in Mombasa

Core legal services delivered from our Mombasa office, tailored to the commercial and property landscape of coastal Kenya.

Real Estate & Property

Title verification, conveyancing, and property dispute resolution in Mombasa and coastal Kenya. Specialist knowledge of coastal leasehold issues under the Land Act (Kenya).

Corporate & Commercial

Company registration, commercial contracts, corporate governance, and business structuring for Mombasa-based enterprises and regional businesses with coastal operations.

Immigration

Work permit applications, entry permits, and immigration compliance for businesses employing expatriates in coastal Kenya and the wider EAC region.

EAC & Cross-Border

EAC Common Market Protocol compliance, Uganda–Kenya–Tanzania corridor legal support, and logistics/trade facilitation agreements for businesses using the Port of Mombasa corridor.

Employment & Labour

Employment contracts, Kenya Employment Act 2007 compliance, HR policy drafting, wrongful termination, and employment dispute resolution for Coast Region employers.

Dispute Resolution

Commercial litigation, arbitration, and mediation for Mombasa-based disputes. Debt recovery, contractual claims, and enforcement of judgments in Kenya's Coast Region courts.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What legal services does M-Smith Advocates offer in Mombasa?

    Our Mombasa office handles corporate law, real estate and property transactions in coastal Kenya, immigration and work permits, employment law, commercial contracts, and EAC cross-border legal matters. We also advise on port and logistics-related commercial agreements.

  • Can the Mombasa office handle property transactions on the Kenyan coast?

    Yes. Coastal Kenya has specific land tenure and property law considerations, including leasehold land issues under the Land Act (Kenya). Our Mombasa team advises on title verification, conveyancing, and property dispute resolution for Mombasa and the wider Coast Region.
