Mining and Mineral Transaction Advisory

Strategic Legal Counsel for Mining Investments, Transactions, and Regulatory Compliance

At M-Smith Advocates, we provide comprehensive legal and transaction advisory services to mining companies, mineral rights holders, investors, financial institutions, contractors, and government stakeholders operating across the mining and extractives sector. Our team combines commercial insight with deep regulatory knowledge to support clients through every phase of mining and mineral transactions, from exploration and licensing to financing, acquisitions, exports, and dispute resolution.

We understand the complex legal, environmental, commercial, and regulatory challenges that shape the mining industry. Our approach is practical, strategic, and tailored to help clients manage risk, secure investments, and achieve sustainable growth in highly regulated and capital-intensive environments.

General information only. This page provides general legal information, not legal advice. Laws change and individual circumstances vary. Contact M-Smith Advocates for advice specific to your situation.

Regional and Cross-Border Experience

Our team supports local and international clients involved in complex cross-border mining and mineral transactions. We are proud to have advised and participated in several successful gold transactions across multiple international markets, including Dubai, Hong Kong, China, the United Kingdom, Malaysia, Singapore, Qatar, South Korea, Japan, Germany, Italy, and the United States.

Our experience in cross-border mineral trade enables us to guide clients through complex international regulatory, commercial, compliance, and transactional requirements with confidence and efficiency. Whether structuring an export agreement, navigating multi-jurisdiction compliance, or advising on cross-border due diligence, we bring direct transactional experience across some of the world's most active mineral markets.

Mining Licences and Regulatory Advisory

We advise clients on obtaining, renewing, transferring, and maintaining mining rights and regulatory approvals. Our team supports compliance with national mining laws, environmental regulations, land use requirements, and local content obligations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What legal support do mining investors require?

    Mining investors often require support with licensing, due diligence, investment structuring, financing, regulatory approvals, ESG compliance, and transaction negotiations. M-Smith Advocates provides end-to-end legal and transaction advisory across the full mining investment lifecycle.

  • Can M-Smith Advocates support cross-border mining transactions?

    Yes. We advise local and international clients on cross-border mining investments, acquisitions, mineral trade, financing, and regulatory compliance across Uganda and the East African region.

  • Do you assist with mineral export licensing?

    Yes. We advise on export permits, compliance requirements, commodity sale agreements, customs matters, and international trade documentation for mineral traders and exporters operating in Uganda and East Africa.

  • Why is legal due diligence important in mining transactions?

    Due diligence helps identify risks relating to mining rights, environmental liabilities, land ownership, regulatory compliance, contractual obligations, and potential disputes before investments or acquisitions are finalised. Thorough due diligence protects investors and enables informed transaction decisions.
