Our Practice Areas

Dedicated legal expertise across eleven specialized domains.

Litigation & Arbitration

We provide full-service representation in court disputes and independent arbitration processes. Our approach is characterized by aggressive advocacy and strategic planning, ensuring that our clients' interests are staunchly protected through every stage of the legal process.

Our team handles commercial disputes, contractual disagreements, and complex civil litigation within the regional courts of East Africa.

Real Estate & Land Transactions

Navigating the property market in East Africa requires local knowledge and meticulous due diligence. We provide expert guidance through the complexities of buying and selling land, specifically focused on eliminating fraud and streamlining the acquisition process.

Our services include title verification, drafting sale agreements, facilitating transfers, and resolving property-related disputes.

Intellectual Property

Your ideas are your most valuable assets. We provide comprehensive legal support for Patents, Trade Marks, and Copyrights. This includes registration, protection strategy, and litigation within the East African region.

We help businesses secure their brands and innovations against infringement, ensuring regional compliance and robust enforcement.

Oil, Gas & Petroleum Law

As the energy sector evolves, we offer specialized consultancy on Exploration, Development, and Production regulations. We help clients navigate national content requirements, sector participation, and complex licensing frameworks.

Mining & Mineral Resources

Handling transactions and legal frameworks related to mineral resource exploration. We advise on mining leases, environmental compliance, and community agreements within the extractives industry.

Employment & Labour

Providing legal counsel on workplace relations, drafting robust employment contracts, and representing clients in labour-related disputes and industrial relations matters.

Corporate & Commercial Services

Our corporate desk provides Company Secretarial services, debt collections, and property management. we act as general legal advisors for businesses, ensuring corporate governance and operational compliance.

Immigration Law

Assisting with residency, visas, work permits, and other immigration-related regulatory matters for expatriates and businesses operating in the region.

Insurance Law

Legal services centered on insurance policy interpretation, complex claims resolution, and providing advisory for both insurers and the insured.

Human Rights

Advocacy and legal support for human rights-related issues, ensuring constitutional protections and international standards are upheld for our clients.

East African Community (EAC) Law

Niche expertise in the regional legal frameworks, cross-border trade legalities, and the evolving protocols within the East African Community (EAC).
